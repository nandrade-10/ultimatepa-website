The process

Simple for your staff.
Thorough on our end.

Your team submits the patient information. We take it from there — building the clinical justification, submitting to the payer, and following up until a decision is reached.

1

Submit patient info

Your staff fills out our secure intake form with the patient demographics, insurance details, medication, and clinical notes. Takes under 5 minutes.

2

We build & submit the PA

Ultimate PA prepares the clinical justification letter and submits the complete PA package to the payer via HIPAA-compliant fax or portal — on your behalf.

3

We follow up

We track every open PA, follow up with payers, and manage denials and appeals. You get notified the moment a decision is made.

4

Track in real time

Your team can check the status of any PA at any time using their unique PA code at our online tracker — no login required.
